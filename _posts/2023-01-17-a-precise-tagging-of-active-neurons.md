活動依存な遺伝学的ラベリング手法のまとめ by 長濱さん：

Immediate early gene-based labeling→temporal resolutionがpoor

IEGs: c-fos, Arc, Egr1, Npas4…e.t.c.
cFos-tTA: [Reijmers et al., Science 2007](https://www.science.org/doi/10.1126/science.1143839):  [Xu et al., Nature 2012](https://www.nature.com/articles/nature11028)

TRAP & TRAP2: Guenthner et al., Neuron 2013 (Miyamichiさんのやつ). Allen et al., Science 2017. iCreERT2を組み合わせて、4-OHT-inducibleに改変。Nativeなfosのシーケンスを壊さないようにinsertしている。
[TRAP: Guenthner et al., Neuron 2013](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S0896627313002729)[TRAP2: Allen et al., Science 2017](https://www.science.org/doi/10.1126/science.aan6747)
[CANE: Capturing Activated Neuronal Ensembles: Sakurai K et al., Neuron 2016](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S0896627316307164)Capturing and Manipulating Activated Neuronal Ensembles with CANE Delineates a Hypothalamic Social-Fear Circuit
from [Fan Wang lab @MIT](https://mcgovern.mit.edu/profile/fan-wang/). 筑波睡眠研の[Katsuyasu Sakuraiさん](https://wpi-iiis.tsukuba.ac.jp/japanese/research/member/detail/katsuyasusakurai/)のお仕事。Activity-dependentにdsTVAを発現するマウスにEnvAを持つPseudotyped ravies viruses (RVs) or lentiviruses (LVs)を補充。ウイルスゆえ、10日以上発現を待つ必要がある。

[E-SARE: enhanced version of synaptic activity-responsive element (Kawashima et al., Nat. Methods, 2013)](https://www.nature.com/articles/nmeth.2559).  Arc-mini promoterの前にSAREを重ねて発現。

[RAM: Robust Activity Marking: Sorensen et al., eLife 2016](https://elifesciences.org/articles/13918) A robust activity marking system for exploring active neuronal ensembles
from [Yingxi Lin lab](https://www.upstate.edu/psych/research/labs/linlab/index.php). AP-1 site (from FOS/JUN family of DNA binding protein) + binding morif of Npas4の組み合わせ。Human Fos-mini promoterの上流に4つtandemに挿入。E-SAREと比較。Labelingのみ。

CaMPARI; Schreiter lab @Janelia →labelingだけで、manipulationができない

[Ver.1 (Fosque et al., Science 2015)](https://www.science.org/doi/abs/10.1126/science.1260922): Photoconvertible fluorescent protein (FP) EosFPを骨格として使用。Green 60 %, Red 40 %の割合で発現。Violet (UV) light (405 nm)でGreen→Redにfluorescent protein のexpressionが変化。active neuronのマーキングが可能。PFA fixationで蛍光が低下
Ver.2 ([Moeyaert et al., Nat. Commun](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41467-018-06935-2)., 2018, 応用例: [Trojanowski et al., Neuron 2020](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.neuron.2020.11.027)): modest PC contrast, slow calcium unbiding, sensitivity to chemical fixatives as formaldehydeなどを改善。
[Reversible switchable (rs)](https://elifesciences.org/articles/57249)(Sha et al., eLife 2020): 480 nm lightとactivityでmark (green fluorescent が低下)。405 nm UVで再度greenが発現。

Ca2+ and light-gated switch system

[Cal-Light](https://www.nature.com/articles/nbt.3902) (Lee et al., Nat. Biotechnol., 2017)
[iTango](https://www.nature.com/articles/nmeth.4234)(Lee et al., Nat. Methods, 2017)  とともに、blue-light-inducible TEV protease (BLITz) systemを用いたin vitro screening系で作製（TEV: tobacco etch virus）。TEV protease cleavage siteとAsLOV2 domainのoptimizationを行っている (Lee et al., Nat. Methods, 2017)。BLITz-1（Alanineが挿入されている）を選択。Tetracycline response element (TRE)でreporter geneの発現をコントロール。

Cal-Lightの使用例：[Park et al., Nature 2021 from Gordon lab and Gogos lab @Colombia Univ.](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41586-021-03272-1)
[Roy et al., P.N.A.S., 2022 from Guoping Feng lab.](https://www.pnas.org/doi/10.1073/pnas.2118712119)
CaMPARIとの比較：[Ebner et al., Frontier in Synaptic Neuroscience, 2017 from Sachdev lab and Larkum lab.](https://www.frontiersin.org/articles/10.3389/fnsyn.2019.00016/full)

[FLARE](https://www.nature.com/articles/nbt.3909)(Fast Light- and Activity-Regulated Expression; Wang et al., Nat. Biotechnol., 2017)
FLAREについては、TEV proteaseをそのままM13側に結合している。Original LOV2 domain (G528A/N538E mutant)にmutationを入れてoptimize、eLOVとして開発。NRX-TM（AAV packagingのため短くした）の後にNav1.6を挿入してsoma-targetingにしている。
[scFLARE Sanchez et al., P.N.A.S., 2020](https://www.pnas.org/doi/10.1073/pnas.2006521117) single component型のFLARE
Updated ver.としてFLiCRE ([Fast Light and Calcium-Regulated Expression; Kim et al., Cell 2020](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.cell.2020.11.015)) [](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.cell.2020.11.015)TEV proteaseのoptimizeでultra TEVp (TEV-S153N mutant)を作製（[Sanchez and Ting, Nat. Methods, 2019](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41592-019-0665-7)）。hLOV1の開発も。
[FLAREのupdateのほかにneuromodulate用のSPARKを作製](https://elifesciences.org/articles/30233)
